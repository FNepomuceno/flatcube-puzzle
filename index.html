FlatCube 3D v0.2.1

Explanation

Some of you might be familiar with the Rubik's Cube puzzle. This puzzle takes a spin on things and introduces Rubik's Cube like puzzles into two dimensions.

There are two kinds of actions possible on FlatCube: re-orienting the cube, and making twists on one of the four sides of the displayed square. Re-orienting the "cube" changes which face is shown. However this can lead to situtaions where you orient the cube multiple times and end up at the face you started at, but with your view rotated. Care must be taken as to not do this when not intended.
